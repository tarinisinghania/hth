Situated below the SACRUM BONE, the awakened MOOLADHARA CHAKRA fills us with innocence and wisdom, bringing with it a joy and wonder devoid of conditionings and prejudice - a quality found in small children. This generally dimishes as we grow and develop ego and self-centered desires. Fortunetly this quality can return, like the sun shining after the passing of heavy clouds. The MOOLADHARA CHAKRA has four petals.
